The Conference

Empowerment and Service

Inspired by the global youth conferences called for by the Universal House of Justice, this gathering brings together around 250 students from diverse backgrounds.

At its heart, a youth conference is highly participatory and reflective. It helps young people:

  • Reflect on their purpose in life
  • Understand their capacity to contribute to their communities
  • Build friendships grounded in shared values
  • Take practical steps toward social action

The gathering begins with a short, thought-provoking opening on purpose and the role of youth, before participants break into small groups to explore meaningful questions about identity, community, and social impact.

Interwoven moments of music, spoken word, and storytelling lift the atmosphere, while the focus steadily shifts from reflection to action. By the end, students actively consider how they can serve. It is an experience where strangers become collaborators, ideas turn into plans, and a few hours spark a sense of direction that lasts far beyond the event.

About YOUTH

Youth Open to Universal Transformation of Humanity

A student-led society empowering youth to walk a path of purpose, service, and transformation through study, reflection, and action. Guided by principles such as unity, justice, and service to the common good, we aim to create a space for meaningful conversations and purposeful friendships, welcoming students of all backgrounds and beliefs.

At YOUTH, members can expect a rich blend of activities throughout the year:
  • Study Circles & Reflection: explore spiritual and social themes through group discussions.
  • Service Projects: engage in initiatives that give back to the community.
  • Speaker Series: learn from professionals who blend career and service.
  • Conferences & Retreats: dive deep into purpose and spiritual rejuvenation.
  • Creative Events: music, art, and storytelling that celebrate identity.
  • Collaborations & Socials: connect with like-minded societies.

Our members aren’t just attendees—they are protagonists in building a more unified and purposeful student culture at UTS. Let’s walk a path of service together.
